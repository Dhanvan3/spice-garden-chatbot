About Us
Spice Garden is an authentic South Indian restaurant established in 2015, known for bold flavors, fresh ingredients, and warm hospitality. We serve traditional recipes passed down through generations, with a modern twist.

FAQs
Do you take reservations?
Yes, we accept reservations via phone or our website. Walk-ins are also welcome based on availability.
Do you offer delivery?
Yes, we deliver within a 5-mile radius. Orders can be placed through our website or via Uber Eats and DoorDash.
Is there a vegetarian/vegan menu?
Yes, we have an extensive vegetarian menu. Vegan options are available — just ask your server or mention it when ordering online.
Do you cater for events?
Absolutely. We offer catering for private events, corporate lunches, and weddings. Contact us at catering@spicegarden.com for a custom quote.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, all major credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.
Do you have parking?
Yes, free parking is available in our dedicated lot behind the restaurant.
Are you halal certified?
Our chicken and lamb are sourced from halal-certified suppliers. Please speak to our staff for more details.
Can I customize my order?
Yes, we accommodate most dietary preferences and allergies. Please inform us when ordering.
What is your cancellation policy for reservations?
We request at least 2 hours notice for cancellations. For large group bookings (8+), 24 hours notice is required.
